Therapeutic focus

Focused enough to be credible. Broad enough to be useful.

Kindrial should not claim to cover everything. The best positioning is early-phase trials where clinical operations, safety oversight, biomarkers, immunology, and CNS complexity require practical sponsor-side judgment.

01

CNS and neurodegeneration

Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, brain injury, cognitive endpoints, biomarker-driven eligibility, imaging vendors, and longer operational timelines.

02

Biomarker-heavy early trials

04

Selected adjacent programs

Focused Phase 1–2a studies in inflammation, metabolic, rare disease, and specialty therapeutics when the protocol fits the model.

Team model

Study teams built around your protocol.

No fake public executive roster. No implied permanent staff that does not exist. Kindrial is positioned as a founder-led virtual CRO with a vetted consultant and vendor network.

Founder-led core

Kindrial is built from sponsor-side clinical development experience: FDA/IRB-governed studies, vendor oversight, cGMP clinical supply coordination, central lab and imaging workflows, DSMB preparation, site startup, and early-phase operational planning.

Senior expert network

Kindrial does not maintain a large permanent staff that sponsors ultimately pay for through overhead. Instead, each study is built around senior consultants, qualified vendors, and clinical operations partners selected for that protocol.

MD

Medical Monitoring

Board-certified physicians engaged by indication, safety profile, and protocol complexity. Support may include eligibility review, AE/SAE review, safety narratives, DSMB preparation, and investigator communication.

CO

Clinical Operations

Senior project managers, CRAs, and site-facing specialists assigned based on site count, geography, enrollment model, and monitoring intensity.

IRB

Regulatory and IRB Strategy

Consultants supporting IND-facing documentation, IRB submissions, protocol amendments, safety reporting workflows, and regulatory communication planning.

BS

Biostatistics

Independent biostatisticians supporting early-phase design, dose escalation logic, interim safety review, DSMB outputs, SAP development, and final analysis.

EDC

Data and Systems

EDC, eTMF, IRT, and reporting systems configured to fit the study instead of forcing sponsors into expensive enterprise infrastructure.

QA

Quality and Compliance

GCP quality support for vendor qualification, TMF oversight, audit readiness, SOP alignment, and essential document review.

Named personnel, CVs, responsibilities, vendor assignments, communication plan, and availability are confirmed during proposal finalization and SOW execution. All specialist roles are assigned subject to project scope, contract execution, consultant availability, and sponsor approval.

Short Mutual Confidential Disclosure Agreement — Intake Version

  1. Parties. The disclosing party and Kindrial may exchange confidential information to evaluate a possible clinical research services engagement.
  2. Purpose. Confidential information may be used only to assess study fit, proposal scope, budget assumptions, vendor feasibility, and possible contracting.
  3. Confidential information. Includes non-public protocol, product, clinical, regulatory, manufacturing, commercial, financial, vendor, and operational information disclosed in writing, orally, visually, or by upload.
  4. Obligations. The receiving party will use reasonable care, restrict access to personnel and consultants with a need to know, and not disclose or use the information outside the purpose.
  5. Exclusions. Confidential information does not include information already known without restriction, publicly available through no fault of the receiver, independently developed, or lawfully received from a third party.
  6. Compelled disclosure. If disclosure is required by law, the receiving party will provide prompt notice where legally permitted and disclose only the required portion.
  7. Term. The confidentiality obligations continue for five years from disclosure. Trade secrets remain protected as long as they qualify as trade secrets under applicable law.
  8. No license or obligation. No IP license, service obligation, partnership, or exclusive relationship is created unless a separate written agreement is signed.
  9. Return or deletion. Upon written request, confidential materials will be returned or deleted, except one archival copy may be retained for legal/compliance purposes.
  10. E-signature. Electronic acceptance may be used as evidence of agreement by the authorized representative.
