The model · Why it works for emerging biotech

A virtual CRO built around
your protocol — not the other way around.

"We don't maintain a large permanent staff that sponsors ultimately pay for through overhead."

✕Bloated staffing model ✕Hidden pass-throughs ✕Quarterly change orders ✓Project-specific teams ✓Senior consultant network ✓Vetted vendor partners

Kindrial operates as a lean virtual CRO. Instead of a large permanent payroll, we build a project-specific study team using senior consultants, qualified vendors, and experienced clinical operations partners — selected for the specific needs of each protocol.

This model gives emerging biotech sponsors access to high-level clinical, regulatory, medical, data, biostatistics, and quality expertise without paying for unnecessary infrastructure. For each awarded study, Kindrial confirms the named project team, CVs, role responsibilities, communication plan, and vendor assignments before study startup.

The result: real Phase I / IIa execution at a price your runway can survive — and a contract you can actually defend to your board.

Kindrial maintains a founder-led core and a vetted network of senior consultants and vendor partners. The named study team for your protocol is confirmed during proposal advancement and SOW execution — based on indication, study design, complexity, and sponsor needs.

This is the standard model for virtual CROs and specialty consulting groups. It keeps overhead off your invoice while giving you access to the right specialists for the work that actually needs to be done.

Medical Monitoring

Board-certified physicians engaged based on indication, safety profile, and protocol complexity. Support may include eligibility review, AE/SAE review, safety narratives, DSMB preparation, and investigator communication.

Clinical Operations

Senior project managers, CRAs, and site-facing specialists assigned based on site count, geography, enrollment model, and monitoring intensity.

Regulatory & IRB Strategy

Consultants supporting IND-facing documentation, IRB submissions, protocol amendments, safety reporting workflows, and regulatory communication planning.

Biostatistics

Independent biostatisticians supporting early-phase design, dose-escalation logic, interim safety review, DSMB outputs, SAP development, and final analysis.

Data & Systems

EDC, eTMF, IRT, and reporting systems configured to fit the study — instead of forcing sponsors into expensive enterprise infrastructure.

Quality & Compliance

GCP quality support for vendor qualification, TMF oversight, audit readiness, SOP alignment, and essential document review.

All specialist roles are assigned subject to project scope, contract execution, consultant availability, and sponsor approval. Upon CDA and proposal advancement, Kindrial provides anonymized capability profiles first, followed by named CVs for proposed key personnel before SOW execution.

Straight answers

Questions
buyers actually
ask us.

Is the $1.95M price truly all-inclusive?+
Yes — under the stated assumptions. CRO services, single-site costs, EDC/eTMF/IRT software licenses, monitoring, regulatory, data management, biostatistics, and the Sponsor Workspace app are all included. The only items scoped separately are listed in our exclusions (cGMP supply, advanced imaging, additional sites, complex assays). If your protocol triggers exclusions, we tell you in the Study-Fit Brief — before contract.
Why only Phase I and early Phase IIa?+
Because focus produces quality. Mid/large CROs spread their attention across Phase I-IV. We exist for emerging biotechs whose entire company depends on a clean first-in-human or early efficacy readout. We don't pretend to compete on global Phase III; we want to be the best Phase I / early IIa partner you'll ever work with.
Do I have to sign a CDA before getting a fit assessment?+
No. You choose. Path A — describe your program in your own words, no CDA, no protocol details required. We give you an indicative fit assessment. Path B — sign a mutual CDA online (e-signature, 2 minutes) and upload your synopsis, IB, or full protocol for a complete assessment. Both paths return a Study-Fit Brief within one business day.
How does the CDA signing work online?+
Standard mutual CDA, biotech-friendly language, e-signature via DocuSign-equivalent. Two minutes from click to signed copy in your inbox. No back-and-forth with our legal. If your legal team wants modifications, we negotiate after the Study-Fit Brief, not before.
What does "virtual CRO" actually mean?+
It means we don't carry a large permanent staff that sponsors pay for through overhead. Instead, we maintain a founder-led core plus a vetted network of senior consultants, qualified vendors, and clinical operations partners. Each project gets a team selected for its specific protocol — confirmed by name, with CVs, before SOW execution. This is the standard model for specialty consulting groups and lets us hold the $1.95M flat price.
Will I know who's actually running my study?+
Yes. Once we move past initial fit and into proposal advancement, you receive anonymized capability profiles for each role; before SOW execution, you receive named CVs for the proposed key personnel — medical monitor, project manager, lead CRA, biostatistician, QA, and regulatory lead. No hidden subcontractors.
Is the Sponsor Workspace really free?+
Yes. No per-seat license, no setup fee, no usage tier. The app is bundled into every Kindrial engagement at zero additional cost — for the entire study lifecycle. Unlimited users on your side.
What if my protocol turns out to require more than the package covers?+
We tell you in the Study-Fit Brief, before any contract. If complex imaging, additional sites, or specialized assays are needed, those are scoped transparently as add-ons with their own fixed prices. The base $1.95M never silently inflates.
What if my study doesn't fit your model at all?+
We say so — in the Study-Fit Brief. "Not a fit" is a real verdict, not a sales tactic. If your program is global Phase III, complex oncology dose-escalation requiring multi-site enrollment, or cell & gene therapy at scale, we'll recommend a larger CRO and probably refer you directly. Our job is to be the right answer, not the only answer.
